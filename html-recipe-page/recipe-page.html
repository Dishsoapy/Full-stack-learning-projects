Butter Chicken Mac n' Cheese

A high protein meal prep recipe I found off instagram. Recipe makes 10 servings

Per Serving:
  • 545 Cal.
  • 42g protein
  • 58g carbs
  • 17G Fat

Ingredients

  • 900g Diced Chicken Breast
  • 2 Onions. Sauteed
  • 30g(2Tbsp)tomato paste
  • 30g(2Tbsp)garlic paste
  • 15g(1Tbsp)ginger paste
  • 30g(2Tbsp)Butter
  • 400g blended roasted-tomatoes
  • 15g sugar or sweetner of your choice

Spice Blend

  • 2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp spicy curry powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • Dash of black pepper

Cheese Mixture

  • 400g plain 0% greek yogurt
  • 120g 1/3 fat cream cheese (or reduced fat)
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Chopped green onion
  • 220g extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • 80g reduced fat mozzarella
  • 1 tsp garam masala

Pasta

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients listed under "Ingredients" into slow-cooker/crock pot and mix.
  2. Slow-cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 3-4 hours
  3. Add in fat free greek yogurt, cilantro, green onions, and mix
  4. Mix in the half cooked pasta of your choice and 1 cup of the pasta water
  5. Add in a garam masala, cheddar, and mozzarella cheese
  6. Mix together all ingredients and let it cook for another 10-15 minutes

Divide into 10 equal servings. Or eat it all by yourself in one sitting and let the wave of guilt wash over you.
